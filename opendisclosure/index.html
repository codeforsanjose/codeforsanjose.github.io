About Code for San Jose

Open Disclosure San Jose is a project of Code for San Jose, a Code for America citizen brigade comprised of a group of volunteers who are passionate about using our skills to advance civic innovation in San Jose.

Our goal is to provide a new way to see campaign contribution data in an accessible and easy to understand format. We hope this visualization tool will be useful in simplifying campaign finance data and shed some light on the money that influences the campaigns of San Jose candidates. We feel that understanding this data keeps government accountable and empowers citizens to understand how campaign funds are raised and spent hereby illuminating the flow of money in politics.

Our data comes from e-filling data provided by the City of San Jose. We want to make this data easier to find, see, and understand. Data is updated as the campaigns submit their latest data according to the filing deadlines set by the California Fair Political Practices Commission (FPPC).
